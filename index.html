Improved returns and better risk management for real estate

KR&A is a European real estate consultancy firm. We offer investors solutions for improved returns, and better risk management. We are experienced in valuing and assessing the outlook for listed real estate companies in Europe. KR&A advises real estate investors about strategy, operations and sustainability. We believe data analytics and the resulting information has become a competitive advantage. We offer a broad range of data solutions for real estate companies. We write bespoke research, we perform due diligence and give second opinions. We are never deal-incentivized. As a result our advice is sound, fully aligned and not clouded by a need for a transaction.

Data solutions

We search, collect, classify, analyse and visualize public and private data to create crucial information for real estate decision-making. This information, its infrastructure and the resulting knowledge and corporate mindset can become a sustainable competitive advantage for real estate companies. KR&A offers you a tailor made real estate data capture and analytics infrastructure. Our data modules focus on the asset level, its catchment area, consumer behavior, transport developments, competing assets, e-commerce or search and social media data. Real estate companies

KR&A is independent

We do not undertake transactions for ourselves or for clients. We do not care if you buy or sell an asset or a company, we do not work on a transaction fee. We do not care if you issue debt or equity, we do not underwrite for a fee. We only care that you have more data, information, knowledge and experience at your disposal than before. That our clients have more intelligence than their counterparts, to better price real estate assets, their risks and returns. We do not tell you what you want to hear, we tell you what you need to know.

Founder

KR&A was founded by Arjan Knibbe. Arjan has been a sell-side real estate analyst since January 1994 at Kempen & Co, UBS Investment Bank and ING. He is a specialist in Continental European property companies and has advised large investors in Europe, the US, Australia and Asia about the strategies, markets, valuation and outlook of the European real estate companies. He has written extensively about pan-European valuation methodologies for real estate investmentment and development companies. He has been involved in a large number of equity raisings and IPOs, across Europe. Arjan has a Master in economy from the University of Amsterdam. He specialised in real estate investment and his thesis about the financial reporting of property companies was awarded a 9/10 score by Prof Kohnstamm. He has been a member of the EPRA best practice committee. Arjan is fluent in Dutch, English, French and German and participated in the INSEAD Transition to General Management program in 2013.
